A book chapter on family business culture

December 11, 2015

After more than a year, my writing on ‘organizational culture and the family business’ is finally published as a book chapter by Edward Elgar. This book is an outcome of a PhD workshop on family business organized by Jönköping International Business School, Sweden. I am deeply thankful to the editors to coin such an idea and, consequently, jump-start the writing process of my PhD (and other fellows’). I believe this is a good starting point to a more interesting discussion on family business in the future.

If you are interested to see some previews, you can view it from Google Books here. Or if you feel like buying it, then it is also available here.
